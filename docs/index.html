Zooom3

Move and resize any window with modifier keys and your mouse. Like X11 and Zooom2, but for modern macOS.

Download for macOS

Move & Resize

Hold your modifier keys and drag anywhere in a window to move or resize it. No need to aim for edges or title bars.

Hover Mode

Just hold your modifiers and move the mouse — no click needed. Feels like the classic Zooom2 experience.

Fully Customizable

Choose your own modifier keys, mouse buttons, and per-app settings from the menu bar.
